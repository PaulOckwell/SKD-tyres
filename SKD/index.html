New & Used Tyres

We Sell new and used tryes for all types of vehicles and wheel sizes. Our used part-worn tryes are carefully selected to ensure pleanty of tread depth without any defects. New tryes are avalible ranging from the very best sport tyres, to all year round tryes proving your vehicle with plenty of grip in all environments. *FREE WHEEL BALANCING*

Do You Need New Tyres?

  • Have a Minimum Depth of 1.6mm
  • Loosing Air In Your Tyre
  • Cuts,Bumps or Threads Exposed
  • Rubber worn evenly all around/not sloaping to one side
